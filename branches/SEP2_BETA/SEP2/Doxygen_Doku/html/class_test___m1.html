Detailed Description

Testing Class for Milestone #1

SE2 (+ SY and PL) Project SoSe 2011

Authors: Rico Flaegel, Tell Mueller-Pettenpohl, Torsten Krane, Jan Quenzel

Tests the Lights and some other hardware stuff. 1. Add lights after another. 2. Turn lights off instantly. 3. Add lights after another. 4. Turn off lights one at a time. 5. Turning on engine in right direction. 6. Turning on engine in left direction. 7. Slowing the engine. 8. Getting back to Normal Speed. 8. Stopping the engine. 9. Setting the engine to slow and left direction. 10. Switching to slow in right direction. 11. Opening the switch. 12. Closing the switch. 13. Resetting the hardware.
